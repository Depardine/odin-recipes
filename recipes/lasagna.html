Lasagna Recipe

Description

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make cheese mixture
  4. Layer lasagna with meat, sauce, cheese.
  5. Cover foil and bake.
  6. Let lasagna rest befor serving.
