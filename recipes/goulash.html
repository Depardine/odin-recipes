Goulash Recipe

Description

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion and Oil
  • Tomato
  • Sauces and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Macaroni

Steps

  1. Cook the onion in oil, then add the ground beef and cook until brown. Add garlic.
  2. Stir in the water, tomatoes, and seasonings. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Stir in uncooked noodles, cover, and simmer until pasta is tender./li>
